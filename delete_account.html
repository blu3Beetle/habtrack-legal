habTrack — Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the habTrack app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

HOW TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

  1. Open the habTrack app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon on the Today page)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete account
  5. Enter your password to confirm
  6. Tap Delete to permanently remove your account

WHAT GETS DELETED

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (display name, email address)
  • All habits and completion history
  • Streak, freeze, and milestone data
  • All data stored in our cloud database (Firebase Firestore)
  • All local app data on the device
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before deleting your account to avoid further charges.
