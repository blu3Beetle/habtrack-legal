PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 15, 2026

INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Notice for habTrack ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our Android habit-tracking application ("Services").

Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support.habtrack@gmail.com.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

What personal information do we process? Your name and email address (for your account), the habits and completion history you create in the app, your subscription status, and basic device/diagnostic data used to run and improve the Service.

Do we process any sensitive personal information? No.

Do we collect any information from third parties? No.

Who processes this data on our behalf? We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics, Analytics) to run the app, and Google Play Billing to process subscription payments. We never see or store your payment card details — Google Play handles that directly.

How do we keep your information safe? Your session credentials are encrypted at rest on your device, all traffic to our backend is encrypted in transit, and we do not log sensitive values. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable technical measures to protect your data.

What are your rights? You can review, correct, or delete your data at any time — including a self-serve "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings that permanently removes your habits, completions, and profile.

How do you exercise your rights? Use the in-app Delete Account option, or contact us at support.habtrack@gmail.com.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name (optional, if you provide one)
  • Email address
  • Password — handled entirely by Google Firebase Authentication; we never see or store your password in plain text

Content you create in the app. Using habTrack means creating your own data, which we store so the app can function and sync across your devices:

  • Habit names, descriptions, emoji, colors, and schedules
  • Reminder times and notification preferences
  • Completion history and streak data
  • Subscription status (whether you're on the free or Pro plan) — this comes from Google Play Billing; we do not collect or store your billing address or card details ourselves

Information Automatically Collected

Some information is collected automatically as part of running the app:

  • Crash and diagnostic data: via Firebase Crashlytics, to help us find and fix bugs (e.g. stack traces, device model, OS version).
  • Basic usage analytics: via Firebase Analytics, such as app opens and screen views, used in aggregate to understand how the app is used. We do not currently offer a dedicated in-app opt-out for this; contact us if you'd like your analytics data excluded.
  • Push notification token: via Firebase Cloud Messaging, so we can deliver reminder notifications to your device.
  • Device data: device model, operating system version, and general technical information needed to run the app correctly.

We use Firebase Analytics (Google's mobile analytics SDK) to understand basic app usage, such as how often the app is opened and which screens are used, in aggregate. For more information on Google's privacy practices, see the Google Privacy & Terms page.

8. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time. If you would at any time like to review or change the information in your account or terminate your account, you can:

  • Log in to your account settings and update your user account
  • Contact us using the contact information provided
  • Users can delete their account and all associated data through the Settings menu using the "Delete Account" feature, or by contacting us at support.habtrack@gmail.com

Upon your request to terminate your account, we will deactivate or delete your account and information from our active databases. However, we may retain some information in our files to prevent fraud, troubleshoot problems, assist with any investigations, enforce our legal terms and/or comply with applicable legal requirements.

Withdrawing Your Consent: If we are relying on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. You can withdraw your consent by contacting us at support.habtrack@gmail.com.

9. ADVERTISING IDENTIFIERS AND TRACKING SIGNALS

habTrack does not use advertising identifiers and does not show ads, so there is no "Limit Ad Tracking" setting that would apply. Some mobile operating systems support Do-Not-Track ("DNT")-style signals, but no uniform standard exists for native apps, so we do not currently respond to such signals.
